About the Academy

Music as a way of life, not just a subject.

The Melodic Monk Academy is a passion-driven music learning space, founded with the vision to make quality music education accessible, engaging, and enjoyable for everyone.

We believe that music is not just a subject — it's an emotion, a way of life. Our approach blends practical learning, personalized mentorship, and performance opportunities so that every student experiences music beyond the classroom.

Started in 2025 at Kathitand, Ratu, Ranchi, the academy follows a structured One-Year Foundation Program designed to build strong musical fundamentals through practical learning, consistent practice, and regular performance opportunities. Upon completing the course and passing The Melodic Monk Performance Assessment, students become eligible to join The Melodic Monk Community and continue their lifelong musical journey.

Founder & Faculty

Meet your instructor.

AK

Ankit Kumar

Founder, Director & Lead Instructor, The Melodic Monk Academy

A passionate, self-trained musician exploring both Indian and Western classical music across Guitar, Keyboard, Flute, Violin, Harmonium, and Cajon. Ankit started the academy to offer personalized, hands-on music learning experiences for students of all ages — fun, practical, and performance-based.

Beyond teaching, Ankit produces and records music from his personal studio, regularly sharing covers and devotional melodies on social media for learners and music lovers to explore and stay inspired.

Courses & Learning Journey

Choose your instrument.

Every student follows the One-Year Foundation Program — 12 months, one class every Sunday, with optional online guidance whenever required.

🎸

Guitar

  • Proper holding, tuning & basic techniques
  • Open string exercises & simple melodies
  • Basic chords & common strumming patterns
  • Bollywood & devotional songs
  • Intro to lead playing & melody picking
  • Performance preparation & confidence building
🎻

Violin

  • Correct posture, holding & bow control
  • Basic note practice & simple scales
  • Simple melodies & devotional tunes
  • Introduction to fingering techniques
  • Rhythm, timing & coordination
  • Group performance pieces & stage confidence
🪈

Flute

  • Holding the flute & proper blowing technique
  • Note clarity, long notes & breath control
  • Basic scale practice & simple melodies
  • Bollywood & devotional tunes
  • Introduction to Alankars (note patterns)
  • Performance practice & confidence building
